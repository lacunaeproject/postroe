Arming the choir.

Jessica Valenti calls her work “arming the choir” — giving the people who already believe in reproductive freedom the facts, language, and history to fight for it. These are the books that did it for her, and for us. Canon, frontline reporting, and the novels that saw it coming.

Designed by Cody Heart with Perplexity and Claude, inspired by Jessica Valenti's Abortion: Our Bodies, Their Lies, and the Truths We Use to Win and her newsletter Abortion, Every Day.
